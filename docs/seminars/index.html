AIEL brown-bag seminar series

A monthly, virtual brown-bag lunch research seminar. From autumn 2025 it is part of the Swedish research cluster on AI, Structural Change and the Future of Work (AISCAF), funded by WASP-HS. Internal or invited external researchers present work-in-progress and get feedback from colleagues.
