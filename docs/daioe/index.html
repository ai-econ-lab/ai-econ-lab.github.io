Data-driven AI Occupational Exposure

DAIOE: how exposed is each job to AI?

DAIOE is the lab's measure of how exposed each occupation is to artificial intelligence, built from data rather than expert guesswork. It is openly published, versioned and mapped across the US (SOC), international (ISCO) and Swedish (SSYK) occupational classifications, so others can use it directly in their own work.
